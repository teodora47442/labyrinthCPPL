enum Mode
Enumerator
KIDS 

If the game begins with the kids mode, the winner is the player who reached all his goals. There is no need for the player to come back to his departure position.

NORMAL 

If the game begins with the normal mode, the winner is the player who reached all his goals and came back to his departure position.
